Chancellor/Empress
The chancellor goes by many names, empress, marshall, knook, and of course, chancellor. It combines the movement of a rook and a knight.
$chancellor-moves.png
The chancellor is represented using the letter E, or a knight with the rook's teethed top-wall beneath it, upside-down.
#Strength
The chancellor is about a half-pawn weaker than the queen. It is stronger than a rook and knight.
