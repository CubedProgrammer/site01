Chess variants are games based on chess but with certain rules changed. They can have new pieces with unique movements, or even be played on boards of other shapes and configurations.

The categories of variants are listed belows, as well as some information pages. You can also use the navigation menu on the left. Only the left side of the table are clickable links.

fairy Fairy pieces and how they move.
square Chess in a grid of square cells.
hexagon Chess in grid of hexagonal cells.
cube Chess in a 3-dimensional grid of cubes.
