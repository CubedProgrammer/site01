Ramping

MOAB-class bloons gain health and speed after round 80, this page is to show you how much health and speed they gain at certain rounds. The table does not go on forever, for higher rounds, use the calculator.

There are toggles on the left side, you can choose to hide the health or speed in the table by clicking on the buttons on the left. You can also toggle the visibility of the calculator there.

Note that this is only for 6.29.0, other versions may be different.
