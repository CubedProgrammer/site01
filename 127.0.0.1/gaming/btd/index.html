Bloons Tower Defense

Refresh

A tower defense game made by Ninja Kiwi. In a game, the player gets money to buy towers that can pop bloons, which grants money, there are also towers that make extra money in the later versions.

The bloons follow a path, if they reach the end, the player takes damage. There are many tracks in the later versions. Beating a track grants rewards like monkey money.
