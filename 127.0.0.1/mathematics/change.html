The above calculator calculates the change in displacement given a certain time, and speed, acceleration, jerk, snap, et cetera.

You may also specify an initial distance. The values can be fractional or negative.

Displacement is equal to speed multiplied by time, acceleration by time squared, jerk by time cubed, snap by time to the fourth, so on and so forth, however, they must be multiplied by a scalar, 1, 1/2, 1/6, 1/24, respectively.
